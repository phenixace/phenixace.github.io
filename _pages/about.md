<div class="intro-section">
I am currently a visiting scholar at <strong>The BlueWhale Lab, National University of Singapore (NUS)</strong>, and a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computing (COMP), <strong>The Hong Kong Polytechnic University</strong> (funded by <strong>HKPFS</strong>). Prior to joining PolyU, I received my Master's degree in Information Technology (with Distinction) from the <strong>University of Melbourne</strong>, under the supervision of Dr. Lea Frermann. I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Information Security from <strong>Shanghai Jiao Tong University</strong> in 2021.
